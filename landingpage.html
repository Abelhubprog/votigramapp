Why Votigram?

Tamper‑Proof Voting

Every vote is recorded on Base L2—immutable, auditable, and verifiable by anyone.

Privacy by Design

Zero‑knowledge proofs keep your ballot secret while ensuring one person—one vote.

Inclusive Access

Join via Web, mobile, USSD/SMS, or offline kiosks—no citizen left behind.

How Votigram Works

1

Create Your Identity

Register with your existing digital ID or create a new pseudonymous identity. Your identity is verified once but remains private in all future interactions.

2

Choose Voting Initiatives

Browse through public participation initiatives, from community proposals to national elections, all secured by blockchain technology.

3

Cast Your Vote

Vote securely from anywhere, with instant confirmation and a tamper-proof record of your participation—without revealing your choice to anyone.

4

Verify Results

See the results tallied in real-time with cryptographic proof. Independently verify all outcomes without requiring trust in any central authority.
